Michael "Mick" White[1]:18 nicknamed "Atari", is an Australian Web Developer, entrepreneur and seriously determined individual, now based in central Germany. Programmer, and website entrepreneur who eagerly searches creative projects to expand his skillset and keep his brain stimulated.
His early career extended broad experience in hospitality management working within accommodation, tourism and event management over twenty years. His prolonged experience working with technology within the tourism and event management sector has seen his interpersonal and communication skills flourish. His abaility to mediate, listen and express to colleaguesa and clients whilst similarly being able to problem solve his way independantly and within team on tech projects makes him invaluable to any organisation.[4]

Early life[edit]

Mick White was born in Melbourne, Australia, the son of James and Eileen White. He is of Australian and English ancestry on his father's side and of the same on his mother's.[5]

The name on White's birth certificate is "Michael Anthony White", but Mick's mother said that she intended it to be spelled "Michael", and "Mick" is what he uses.[1] :18

White has been referred to frequently by the nickname "Mick", or "Atari";[7]. The city of Melbourne plan to name a street "Mick Street" in his honour awaiting funding from the federal Government or special dispensation from the Her Majesty The Queen ☺.

UbCool.it[edit]

Origins of UbCool.it[edit]

In 1999, White began his experience installing, maintaining and configuring the local aea network and desktop machines, within the three business sites of the Flying Pig Groep in Amsterdam Netherlands. As the Company liason with external IT support in the evnt of systems failures White maintained business systems for 3 years, before returning to Australia. Upon his return Mick continued to work in Hospitality and Tourism and was often called upon to advise and implement business networks for respective businesses over the proceeding 15 years. Simultaneously, Mick has authored and maintained company websites predominantly usng HTML and css. In more recent times his skill set has broadened to inclde JAVA and javascript, Python, Kivy, SQL. Mick strives and continues to learn more. Mick is currently enrolled at James Cook University, Queensland where he he is soon to complete his Information Technology Degree.

"Mick has been making himself useful since the day he was born..."[12]

In 2013, Mick created the www.ubcool.it domain as an unbrella for various personal and enetrprise projects. in the interests of privacy and security, he operates a variety of domains and pointers and publishes work for business and personal demands. [15]
